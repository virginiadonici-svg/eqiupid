Privacy Policy

Last updated July 19, 2026

What NurseLens does

NurseLens lets a nurse point their phone camera at a piece of medical equipment or supply and get a plain-language explanation of what it is and how it is generally used. It is an orientation reference only, not clinical decision support.

What we collect

When you capture or choose a photo, that image is sent once to Anthropic's Claude API for identification. NurseLens does not have user accounts, does not collect your name or contact information, and does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

How photos are handled

  • Each photo is used for a single identification request and is not stored on NurseLens's servers.
  • Photos are processed by Anthropic (the maker of Claude) to generate the identification result. See Anthropic's own privacy policy for how they handle API data.
  • Keep patients and any patient-identifying information out of frame before you capture a photo.

Camera access

NurseLens requests camera access only to let you capture a photo of an item. You can also choose an existing photo instead of using the camera.
